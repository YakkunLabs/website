Our Company

Meet the team and values behind Yakkun Labs

About Us

Yakkun Labs is a Sri Lankan startup pushing the boundaries of Web3 gaming. We build tools and platforms that bridge the Web2 to Web3 gap with immersive game-first experiences. Our team is passionate about blockchain technologies and the future of decentralized gaming.

Our Team

Composed of developers, artists, and blockchain experts. We believe in empowering creators and communities through game-driven innovation. Our core team is based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, and is committed to shaping the future of Web3 gaming from this vibrant, growing tech hub.

Careers

We are always looking for bold thinkers and Web3 believers. Join our mission to redefine digital play, ownership, and reward systems. Yakkun Labs is based in Sri Lanka, a country that has rapidly embraced blockchain innovation and is well-poised for the Web3 revolution.

Investors & Partners

Collaboration is in our DNA. We work with passionate investors and studios aligned with building open, secure, and sustainable ecosystems. Our strategic partnerships span across local and international Web3 ecosystems, and we are always looking to expand our network.

Events

Catch us at upcoming gaming, blockchain, and innovation expos where we showcase our tools and titles. We actively participate in events both in Sri Lanka and globally to stay ahead of industry trends and share knowledge with the broader Web3 community.
